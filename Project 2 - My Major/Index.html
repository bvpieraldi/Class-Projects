Why I Chose This Major

I chose this major because I’m a creative person and very "artsy". I originally was sticking to majoring in graphic design. last Fall at AACC I took my first Web Design class as a requirement. I then continued to enjoy it and took my 2nd web design class. I started to learn more and more CSS and HTML and even an introduction to JavaScript. As a graphic designer I like making logo’s and designs. Then, I was fascinated by the logical and organization of designing through HTML and CSS. Throughout school my best “real subject” was always math. I enjoy math and the problem solving of I but, nothing I would want to major in. I think HTML and CSS sort of has that same logic and problem solving aspects, making it “mathematical” in some ways.

This does make me think even more about computer programming in general since it does involve good math skills. I also enjoy UX and creating wireframes! BEAWRE: Nerdy
